Unlink Your Account

Remove your Roblox verification from all Trizly servers. This action will disconnect your Discord account from your Roblox account.

⚠️ Warning: This will remove your verification and verified roles from all servers using Lumi.

What happens when you unlink:

• Your Roblox account will be disconnected from your Discord

• All verified roles will be removed

• You can re-verify at any time

• This action is immediate and cannot be undone

Cancel

You will be asked to sign in with Discord to confirm your identity before unlinking.

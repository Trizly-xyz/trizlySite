What is Trizly?

Trizly is a development group constructing high-performance Discord automation and Roblox verification systems designed for ERLC and similar communities. Everything is coded from scratch — designed for speed, security, and scalability.

  • Modern automation: sessions, moderation, tickets, blacklists, logging, affiliations, giveaways, and more.
  • Roblox verification ecosystem: full API, Discord integration, identity pipeline, and developer tools.
  • Unified backend: bots, APIs, dashboards, and services all connected through a single central system.
